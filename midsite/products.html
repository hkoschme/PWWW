Lemon

Lemon Sanctimonious Sparkling Water! will blow your mind. It is so righteous, but in a pretentious way! It has an obvious lemon flavor with an enjoyable fizz. The lemon isn't subtle, but it isn't overpowering either. For an added kick, enjoy it with the juice of half a lemon and a tablespoon of sugar: your own customized lemonade that even Beyoncé would be jealous of. Check it out at Kroger!


Lime

Lime Sanctimonious Sparkling Water! was our second flavor ever created, but some people say it's better than the first. We'll let you be the judge of that! Similar to the lemon, the lime will sweep you off your feet and over the threshold of a new home. Seriously, you will love it as much as newlyweds love each other. Lime goes well with many occasions, even, and especially, when you are having an uppity first argument with your significant other. Find it at Pizza Hut, when you are ordering post-wedding pizza!


In Development: Lemon-Lime

This fruity blend will be a combination of our two most popular flavors, lemon and lime. We invite you to give it a try on your next trip to Costco, the one place married couples can agree on.
