As a Statistics and Machine Learning Major, I have had the opportunity to work on many projects, both in groups and by myself. I pride myself on the work that I have done, and I look forward to working with you in the future!

Does lower windspeed lead to a larger number of hourly users?
In this report, I hypothesize that a lower windspeed corresponds to more bike users per hour. Using extensive analysis, I discovered that there is an acceptable simple linear relationship between the windspeed and the number of bikes rented out at that hour. Yet, it does not accord with my hypothesis.
An Analysis of Sharing in Social Media: What do we choose to share?
In this report, I took several factors of articles that were shared on social media,  and hypothesized that shorter articles with more links, negativity, and images would be shared more. I concluded that negativity is the most important factor.
